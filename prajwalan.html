Central India's Biggest Technical Festival
About

Prajwalan, the technical festival of GCOEA has grown into Indias most anticipated technical festival. From raising funds to organizing events, it's completely structured and shaped by students only. What sets prajwalan apart from other technical festival is devotion of students working for it and the urge to deliver the maximum. The Competitions, events, and workshops have attracted nearly 15,000 students from all over the country participating & showcasing their talents and excelling in their field.
